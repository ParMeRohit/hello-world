Career After BSC Physics

Radiation Oncologist

A Radiation Oncology is the use of ionizing radiation in the treatment of cancer also called Radiotherapy. In the treatment of Cancer, it is one of the primary specializations apart from medical and surgical oncology. A candidate needs to finish their Masters or Doctorate degree in Medical School and also obtain the certificate and license in order to pursue the career of Radiation Oncologist. You will find the best state-of-the-art technology for Cancer treatment using Radiotherapy at Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital in Mumbai.

Radiologist

Radiology requires an extensive study in Medical Institution for minimum 4 years or more after graduation depending upon the specialization. A Radiologist helps determine or diagnose and give results of injuries and illness through the medium of medical imaging techniques such as X-rays, Ultrasounds, Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI), computed tomography (CT) and others. Radiologist is a highly sought after profession.

Aviation Industry

The "avis" from the word Aviation literally translates to "bird n Latin. Hence any business dealing with Air travel is included as a part of Aviation Industry. Aviation Industry is mainly about manufacturing, operating and maintaining the Aircrafts. There are numerous job opportunities in Aviation, such as being a Pilot, flight Attendant, Avionics Technician, Ground/Airport staff and Aviation Meteorologist to mention a few. India is a fast growing market in Aviation Industry.

Agricultural research services

An individual working in an Agricultural Research Services or ARS also known as Scientist works to research and produce better ways of production and prevention of agriculture, fisheries, agroforestry,home science, animal husbandry and allied science. These scientists' work in teams towards making our nation a self sufficient country in the production of crops, food grains, milk, fish, poultry with the increasing challenges concerning the growing population. After clearing the examination of Prelims, Mains and Interview, one has to get enrolled in the Foundation Course for Agricultural Research Service (FOCARS) in Hyderabad.

Construction firms

A Construction Physicist, has to calculat important factors related to indoor and outdoor climate, heat and moisture transfer and fire insulation of structural components. It can also be defined as 'Building Physics' which is an applied science that teaches in theory and simple calculations to evaluate building construction requirements. In construction firms, the job could include to build the correct foundation of a building to be constructed using physics, to evaluate materials and components used in the building constructions and other such works.

Demolition Squads

Demolition squads work include dealing with explosives and and working to dismantle, deploy, repair, destroy and detonate constructions in purpose of new buildings, and mining caves and other such structures. Sometimes, when working in explosive demolition field, one is required to attain a 'Blasters License' Certificate. One needs to have knowledge about materials such as lumbar, brick, rock etc. This job is for professionals who enjoy the wear and tear of big structures with the use of explosives and heavy machinery.

Pyrotechnics manufacturers

'Pyro' in greek stands for 'fire' and hence pyrotechnicians learn to deal with the use of fire in different ways. A pyrotechnicians job is to use exothermic chemical reactions to contain the heat and explosive material in a way to create an explosion or fire. This mechanism is widely used in the making of firecrackers, oxygen candles and also to create gas-pressured blasts in mining and demolition. In the recent times this knowledge has been helpful in creating visual effects in concerts,sports arena and for special effects in movies too.

Consultant Physicist

Physicist are curious about nature about everything that exists. Physicist work to research and develop on matters that are of importance and betterment of mankind, such as to test theories and lab test them. As a Consultant Physicists your job may include to work in an organisation within a team, and consult them on development of machinery or other matters concerning with the success of technological products. One can also be a professor in Institutions on the subject of Physics.

Research associate

A Research associate's job is to research, analyze and to gather data to provide the concerned team with information on a particular topic in an organization. Initially an undergraduate or a graduate needs to work as an apprentice under a professor or researcher to gather experience and build a career in this field. As a Physics Research Associate, one's job will be to research on topics related to Physics only.

Benchmark

Sir Isaac Newton PRS (25 December 1642 – 20 March 1726/27) was an English mathematician, physicist, astronomer, theologian, and author (described in his own day as a "natural philosopher") who is widely recognised as one of the most influential scientists of all time, and a key figure in the scientific revolution.

Niels Henrik David Bohr ( 7 October 1885 – 18 November 1962) was a Danish physicist who made foundational contributions to understanding atomic structure and quantum theory, for which he received the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1922. Bohr was also a philosopher and a promoter of scientific research.

Albert Einstein was a German-born theoretical physicist, best known for his Special and General Theory of Relativity and the concept of mass-energy equivalence expressed by the famous equation, E = mc2. He received the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921
