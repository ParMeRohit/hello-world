Badminton



This racquet sport, which came into eye of world in 19 th century soon become favourite among the masses. This game is played with ‘Singles’ as well as ‘Double’. Badminton also promotes physical fitness, which helps us to reduce anxiety and stress. It is been said that this sport is an exercise itself, which helps players to maintain good, sleep that also impacts in good academics. As Badminton has gained good momentum in India, there are various institutes in all states, which are nourishing players to represent country on world stage.
