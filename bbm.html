Bachelor Of Business Management(BBM)

Bachelor of Business Management is an undergraduate degree offered by many Universities in India. The course is aimed at providing students with skills and knowledge required to take up challenging careers in business and industry, thus enabling them to succeed as professionals.

BBM provides specialization in a variety of areas such as Human Resources, operation, finance, and marketing.

Career After Bachelor of Business Management

Business Development Manager

The role and responsibilities of a business development manager is to bring more business to the company through all means possible. It is a position of importance to the organisation hence it could be a commision based, lucrative job too. This job involves staying in constant touch with the old and new clients, preparing and presenting new project presentations and hereby bringing new business to the organisation. This job could make one travel to different cities or even countries for meetings, conferences and industry events in order to pitch projects and acquire business for the organisation.

Relationship Manager

People with Interpersonal communication along with keen analytical and research skills could have a flourishing career as a Relationship Manager. It would be an added bonus to be an extrovert in nature as this would help in maintaining positive relationships with the clients. Relationship Manager requires one to build, foster and maintain healthy professional relationships also to grab opportunities to up-sell and cross-sell to existing clients. Relationship manager take care of any complaints and problems faced by the clients and solves them in a professional manner.

Business Analyst

Most time a Bachelor's Degree in Business Management is sufficient however some companies may ask for a master's Degree to work for them. This job profile has been discovered due to its need in the 21st century. A Business Analyst's job is to take a technical approach and analyse company problems in the constantly changing times for a profitable outcome to the company. This job requires a lot of planning and documenting business requirements and then analysing them to fulfill business needs cost-effectively. Also the planning and execution is to be communicated clearly with stakeholders, facilitators and partners.

Delivery Manager

A Delivery Manager is a job role started just a couple of years back with the onset of online orders and deliveries. A Delivery Managers's job is to manage time and workforce in a manner to deliver more and increase the productivity. A Delivery Manager analysis and foresees any problems that may hinder timely deliveries and plans accordingly with the team. Also ensures that a positive environment is maintained in the offices and with customers.

Business Operations Manager

Business Operations Manager's role includes tracking daily business results and eventually analysing to increase productivity with implementing required process. Business Operations Manager works in and for various departments in a company. It requires one to be exceptionally good in management and communication skills to coordinate with HR and others to meet business requirements. Operations department is the heart of a company and hence the role of Business Operations Manager is of vital importance. Apart from hiring of candidates, addressing budget matters, contracts and team projects are some of its responsibilities.

Associate Manager

Associate Manager needs to be skilled in clerical and managerial duties. Duties include allocating responsibilities to the team members as per their job profiles to ensure the smooth functioning of the daily processes in the office. An Associate Manager trains the team to carry out daily duties such as answering phones, typing reports, letters, faxing documents and greeting customers. Some of the most important skills required to be an Associate Manager is strong organizational qualities, communication and customer service skills with qualities such as confidence, patience and a professional attitude.
