Career After Bachelor in Computer Applications

Programmer

A computer programmer's job is to code and decode the designed programs into computer languages for proper functioning of the programs and apps developed by the software designers and engineers. This job is achieved after pursuing degree with graduation in Information Technology along with doing entry level jobs for a couple of years to prove the experience. It increases the market value of a student to be certified in specific computer languages such as Java, C++, Python, PHP, etc.

Tester

As a software tester, one's job is to test computer programs or software designs for any errors or bugs with functional testing, customer scenario testing, data loading testing and many more automatic and manual test, at the programs final stages. Software tester has to make sure that the client and customers receive the functioning of the final product as intended and faultless. This technical role requires a strong understanding of SQL, a database language.

Computer support specialist

As a Computer Support Specialist you are required to troubleshoot and resolve multiple computer and software issues. This job could be limited to help desk or even as an employee in IT department of an organization. Further variation in this job could also have one working as a Computer Network Support Specialist troubleshooting Local Area Network (LAN's), Wide Area Networks (WAN's) and Internet systems by door-to-door service. Though same job profiles yet the Computer Support Specialists' are also known as Technical or Tech Support Specialist, Help Desk Technician, IT Specialist, Network Technician.

Hardware and Network Expert

With the increased use of desktops, laptops, mobiles and Internet in private and government organizations, Computer Hardware and Networking Expert is one of the most lucrative careers in recent times. Large-scale organizations need to handle their host systems on a daily basis in the LAN and WAN networks to keep smooth exchange of data between their computers and devices, for which they are highly dependent on Hardware and Networking Expert. Traditionally a Hardware and Network professionals works on Motherboards, RAM, Modems, Routers, Firewalls and Wireless Access Points and other such devices.

G-CAT Master

This is a 1 year course at the Graphiti School of Animation, Mumbai. Candidate needs to have a Diploma or Degree in Arts field or clear the Entrance Exam for admission. This course emphasizes on the creativity using computer graphics and modern technology to bring imagination to life. One gets to work on high-end three-dimensional software packages and tools to become well-trained digital artists. Professionals after completing G-CAT have good career options in the entertainment industry.

Program in Information Security Management

Program in Information Security Management is a study of procedures and policies to secure the sensitive data of a company and/or an organisation from threats by systematically managing and ensuring the protection of the data confidentiality. After completing this course, the candidate has an opportunity to work with multinational companies in the Information Security Management department. This is a very vital and important role in any organisation.

Further Studies

Master’s Degree in Information Management(MIM)

Masters in Information Management (MIM) is a new addition of the existing courses in Information Technology. This course is a combination of theory and practical problem-based learning. MIM degree holders have a bright career path in employment areas such as Banking sector, Management consultancy, Technology, Multimedia, Telecom and few others with job types being a Business Analyst, IT manager, Software Consultant and others similar.

Master of Computer Applications(MCA)

Masters in Computer Application is a 3 year post graduation degree to be pursued after graduation in Bachelor of Computer Application or in any related field. It is an in depth advanced study of Computer Applications with emphasis on latest programming languages and tools to develop better and faster applications in Information Technology field . Admission for this course if usually dependent on the entrance exam or the marksheet of graduation degree. The fee for this course varies between 1 to 3 lacs per annum as per the University. However after Masters in Computer Application one can secure a job in top IT companies like Wipro, TCS and Accenture and earn upto 2.5 to 3.5 lacs per annum.

Post Graduate Program in Corporate Studies(PGPCS)

In this generation, it is survival of the fittest and hence this program is pursued after graduation by the candidates to take a leap in the fast growing career and economic opportunities. This program of approximately 2 years, teaches and enables the candidate to take on Team Leading or Managerial posts in various areas such as Accounting, Marketing, Management and Human Resources, etc. This program gives the candidates leverage in job interviews and also improves their marketability. This program is available at International School of Corporate Management (ISCOM), Pune.

Post Graduate Diploma in Management

To reach the top of the corporate ladder this PGDM program teaches about developing business, management and communication skills. This course can be pursued only after scoring a minimum 50% in graduation exam. Fees for this program could be as less as 16,500, depending on Institutes. Minimum duration for this program could be 1.5 years to a maximum of 8 years. This program enables candidates to become world-class leaders with practical knowledge on problem solving skills and team building analytics taught through this course.

Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Applications

Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Applications is a more elaborate Study of Computer Science after graduation in similar field. This course can be of 1 to 3 years depending on the Institute. Similarly admission fees could be between 2000 to 18 Lacs rupees. Many a times these admission are cleared only after passing Entrance test such as ISI Admission test. After this course, a candidate can build careers in Educational Institutes, Research Firms, Hospitals, Health Care and such others. Later, depending on experience and expertise, annual income could be between INR 2 to 10 lacs.
